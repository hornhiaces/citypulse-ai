# 🎬 City Pulse AI — Pitch Video Script (2-4 minutes)

## Recording Tips
- Screen record the live app at https://city-pulse-aip.lovable.app
- Use a clear, confident voiceover
- Show BOTH Leadership and Citizen modes
- Demo the AI Briefing with a real question
- Keep transitions smooth — the app is the star

---

## Script (~3 minutes)

### Opening — The Problem (0:00–0:25)
**[Show: Overview dashboard loading]**

> "Every day, cities like Montgomery generate thousands of data points — emergency calls, service requests, business licenses. But this data sits in spreadsheets and silos, invisible to the leaders who need it and the citizens who deserve it."
>
> "What if a city could think? What if data could speak?"

### Introduce the Solution (0:25–0:50)
**[Show: City Pulse AI logo + Overview page with KPIs]**

> "This is City Pulse AI — an AI-powered municipal intelligence platform that transforms raw city data into real-time insights. It serves two audiences: city leaders who need to make smarter decisions, and citizens who want transparency into how their city operates."

### Data Collection (0:50–1:20)
**[Show: Navigate to Data Upload page, show file selection]**

> "We ingest real municipal data — 311 service requests, 911 emergency calls, and business licenses. Our pipeline auto-detects dataset types, processes them in chunks, and deduplicates records automatically."
>
> **[Show: Transparency page with dataset catalog]**
>
> "Every dataset is cataloged and made available through our transparency portal."

### Analysis & Intelligence (1:20–2:00)
**[Show: Safety page → district scoring → heatmap]**

> "Our compute engine scores every district across five dimensions — public safety pressure, emergency demand, economic activity, infrastructure stress, and community engagement."
>
> **[Show: Heatmap on Map page]**
>
> "This feeds into a real-time heatmap showing where the city needs attention most."
>
> **[Show: Recommendations page]**
>
> "And it generates AI-powered action recommendations — prioritized by urgency and grounded in actual data signals."

### AI Briefing Demo (2:00–2:40)
**[Show: Navigate to AI Briefing page]**

> "But the most powerful feature is this — our AI briefing engine. Powered by Google Gemini with Retrieval-Augmented Generation, leaders can ask natural language questions and get streaming, data-grounded answers."
>
> **[Type and submit: "What are the most urgent public safety concerns across Montgomery districts?"]**
>
> **[Show streaming response]**
>
> "Every response is backed by real data stored in pgvector — no hallucination, just intelligence."

### Dual Mode (2:40–3:00)
**[Show: Toggle from Leadership to Citizen mode]**

> "And because civic transparency matters, citizens get their own view — same data, plain language, complete openness."

### Closing — Impact & Vision (3:00–3:20)
**[Show: Overview page with all KPIs]**

> "City Pulse AI is production-ready today. Built on Supabase and Google Gemini, it's designed to scale from one city to thousands. Because every city deserves to think smarter — and every citizen deserves to see how."
>
> "Thank you."
